Password Reset

To be able to log in to the Student Information System, you need to click on the "Reset Password" button located on the side of the screen. For password reset, you can use either the "Reset with Identification Information" or "Reset with Email Address" options. After entering the necessary information, when you click on the "Reset Password" button, your password will be sent to the email address registered with ÖSYM. Your username is your student number.
